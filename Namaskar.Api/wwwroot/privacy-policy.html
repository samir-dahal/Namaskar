Namaskar — Nepali New Tab

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective date: June 1, 2026

Overview

Namaskar is a browser extension that replaces your new tab page with a beautiful Nepal photography background, the current Bikram Sambat (BS) date, Nepali festivals and tithis, and an optional local weather widget.

We collect no personal data. We run no analytics. We serve no ads. This policy explains in plain language what the extension does and does not do with information on your device.

Data we collect Nothing

Namaskar does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, or account identifiers are collected.
  • No browsing history, search queries, or page visit data are collected.
  • No keystroke or click-stream analytics are recorded.
  • No crash reports or telemetry are sent anywhere.

Data stored on your device

The following information is saved in your browser's local storage using the standard browser.storage.local API. It never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than the extension itself.

  • Display name — an optional first name shown in the greeting (e.g. "Good morning, Sita.").
  • City name — the city you enter for weather display.
  • Geographic coordinates — latitude and longitude derived from the city you choose during setup, used solely to fetch weather data.
  • Preferences — language choice (English / Nepali), and toggles for the weather and tithi widgets.
  • Daily cache — a one-hour cached copy of the Nepali date, events, and background photo URL to avoid redundant network requests.

You can delete all stored data at any time by removing the extension.

Network requests

The extension makes two types of outbound requests:

1. Namaskar API (namaskar.runasp.net)

Once per hour, the extension fetches today's Nepali date, festival events, tithi, and a background photo URL from our own API. No user data is included in this request — it is a plain unauthenticated GET request identical for every user.

2. Open-Meteo weather API (api.open-meteo.com)

If you enable the weather widget, the extension sends your latitude and longitude (which you provided voluntarily) to Open-Meteo to retrieve the current temperature and weather condition. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather service that requires no API key and does not log or sell user data. No other information is sent in this request.

Background photos

Background images are sourced from Pexels via our server-side API and served as plain image URLs. The extension displays photographer attribution as required by Pexels' license. No user data is sent to Pexels.

Permissions explained

  • storage — saves your settings and daily cache locally in the browser. Nothing is synced to any server.
  • alarms — triggers a once-per-hour background refresh of the daily data cache. No user-visible actions are taken.
  • https://api.open-meteo.com/* — allows the weather request described above. This host permission is required by the browser to make cross-origin requests from an extension.

Third-party services

The extension interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo — Terms & Privacy
  • Pexels (indirectly, via our API) — Privacy Policy

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Sentry, or any other analytics or error-tracking service.

Children's privacy

Namaskar does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
